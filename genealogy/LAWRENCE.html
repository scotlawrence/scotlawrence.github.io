Page 2, the LAWRENCE family of Waverly, NY.

This page is my Paternal Grandfather's ancestors and relatives, starting with my Father's Father,
my Grandfather (4) Daniel Alfred LAWRENCE.

My Lawrence family and ancestors have all lived in Tioga County, New York (Waverly, Nichols, Spencer, VanEtten)
since the 1850's, and in Waverly specifically since the early 1900's. My LAWRENCE line is descended from Lawrence's
of the New York City area, and can be traced back to England. The ACKLES and HUGG line can be traced back to
Spencer, NY around 1800. We are long-time residents of the area!

Actually, both my Father's and Mother's family have lived in "The Valley" for 100 to 200 years, depending on the
specific family line. "The Valley" is a community that sits right on the NY-PA border, Waverly is in NY state,
and Sayre and Athens are in Pennsylvania. My Dad's line (LAWRENCE, ACKLES, HUGG)) are from north of the state line, Tioga County, NY, and my Mom's line, KREMER and TALLADA, are all from just south of the line,
Bradford county, PA (Athens, Sayre, Towanda, etc)


Im proud to be a "Valley Native"! descended from a long line of Valley Natives..
