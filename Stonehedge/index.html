Welcome to the STONEHEDGE RAILWAY webpage!

The Stonehedge Railway is a Garden Railroad built by Scot Lawrence, in Western NY, USA. 

My Stonehedge Railway gets its name and inspiration from my Dad, who built the original Stonehedge & Shady Pines Railroad in Waverly, NY between 2001 and 2009. (click here for my Dad's railroad)

In addition this webpage, which you are currently reading, I also have a "builders log" thread on mylargescale.com. The builder's log on MLS somewhat duplicates this page, but it also contains all the input from the MLS crew, which is very valuable..check out my MLS builders log here.

Thanks everyone!
