2013 - Year Four - Some more backfilling, and completion of the fence/backdrop.

I spent all of 2013 in College! I started at Monroe Community College in September 2012, was there all of 2013, and will graduate in the Spring of 2014.

Even though I wasnt working, small bits of work did get done on the garden railroad...some backfilling with dirt, and the big project for the summer was the completion of the wood fence along the back border of the railroad. I built the fence panels from scratch, using pressure-treated wood fence slats from Home Depot and 2x4 stringers.
